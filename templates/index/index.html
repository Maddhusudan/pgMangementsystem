About Us

At Baba PG, we believe a peaceful stay builds a powerful day. Located in the heart of Bikaner, we offer clean, safe, and well-managed accommodation for students and working professionals. With fully furnished rooms, high-speed Wi-Fi, regular housekeeping, and 24/7 security, Baba PG provides a home-like environment equipped with modern amenities. Our prime location near colleges, public transport, and daily essentials ensures complete convenience. We also bring technology into your stay — from online rent payments to maintenance support, everything is just a click away. Whether you're here for a month or a year, we promise comfort, care, and hassle-free living. Baba PG – Where comfort meets convenience, and every guest feels at home.
